== Description ==

Crypto Token is a stylish and modern WordPress theme designed specifically for cryptocurrency and blockchain businesses. With its sleek and professional design, this theme is the perfect platform to showcase your services, products, and projects in a way that will grab your audience's attention. The theme features a clean, responsive layout that is optimized for all devices, making it ideal for both desktop and mobile users. Additionally, it includes a variety of customization options that allow you to personalize your site to match your brand and style preferences. With Crypto Token, you can create a memorable online presence that reflects the cutting-edge technology of your industry. Go Check Demo: https://wpfrank.com/demo/crypto-Token/

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
